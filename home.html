Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, English Janala is completely free for all learners to access basic vocabulary and lessons.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No, you can start learning immediately without any registration. However, creating an account helps track your progress.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

Practice our daily vocabulary cards, listen to pronunciations, and try to use the words in your daily conversations.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Currently, we focus on self-paced learning. Certificate features may be added in our future updates.
